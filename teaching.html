My Teaching Philosophy

A conecept which is at the core of my philosophy is the belief that education should equip students with skills and also the ability to apply them correctly with critical thinking, while enabling them to empower them to create an ethical and sustainable future.

In my lesson i include culturally relevant examples and methods, considerations, and social and environmental awareness into my lesson and curriculum design.

The goal or aim i wish to achieve is for my students to leave my classroom with knowledge, curiosity, purpose, and a strong sense of direction
